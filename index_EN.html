EMPOWERING EDUCATORS FOR SUSTAINABLE FUTURE:
ENHANCING TEACHER TRAINING
CAPACITY FOR
UKRAINE'S RECOVERY

Project: 101237551 — EESF — ERASMUS-EDU-2025-CBHE

Project goals and objectives

1.

To develop a new academic course
“Education for Sustainable Development” and integrate sustainability-related modules into existing curricula.

2.

To design and produce innovative teaching and learning materials in the field of Education for Sustainable Development.

3.

To enhance the professional competences of secondary school teachers through the implementation of structured professional development programmes in sustainable development.

4.

To strengthen the professional capacity of academic and administrative staff of higher education institutions through targeted training focused on sustainability-related competences.

5.

To reinforce cooperation between Ukrainian and European educational institutions through partnerships, joint training activities, research collaboration, and study visits.

6.

To promote digital transformation in teacher education through the development and implementation of online courses in Education for Sustainable Development.

7.

To ensure the sustainability and institutional integration of project results within partner universities.

8.

To develop (enhance) and implement university Gender Equality Strategies and organise dedicated seminars for academic and administrative staff.

9.

To establish SDG University Labs as academic platforms for integrating the Sustainable Development Goals into research, education, and innovation activities of universities.
